FAQ

Good to know.

How far in advance should I book?

For bridal services, I recommend booking 6–9 months ahead. For all other services, 2–4 weeks is ideal, though last-minute requests are always welcome based on availability.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes — bridal trials are highly recommended and can be booked separately. We'll explore looks together and refine every detail before your big day.

What's your deposit & cancellation policy?

A 30% non-refundable deposit secures your date. Cancellations made at least 48 hours in advance can be rescheduled within 60 days. No-shows or late cancellations forfeit the deposit.

Do you travel?

Absolutely. I offer mobile service throughout the local area, with a small travel fee applied based on distance. Destination weddings are quoted individually.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Arrive with clean, moisturized skin (no makeup), and bring inspiration photos if you have them. Avoid waxing or facials within 48 hours, and stay hydrated.

What products do you use?

I use a curated kit of premium, long-wear, and HD-friendly products from professional brands — clean, hypoallergenic options are available upon request.
