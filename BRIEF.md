## 8. Design Direction

### Color Palette
| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background base | Warm white | `#FAF7F2` |
| Soft accent | Blush pink | `#F3DCD2` |
| Highlight | Champagne | `#E8D5B7` |
| Neutral | Beige / nude | `#D9C5A8` |
| Text primary | Deep mocha | `#3B2C26` |
| Text muted | Warm taupe | `#7A6A5E` |
| Luxe accent | Soft gold | `#C9A36A` |

### Typography
- **Display headings:** *Cormorant Garamond* or *Playfair Display* — elegant serif, italic accents
- **Body:** *Inter* or *Montserrat* — light weight, generous line-height
- **Accent / labels:** Letter-spaced uppercase for refinement

### Visual Language
- Generous whitespace
- Soft, blurred imagery transitions
- Thin gold dividers and underlines
- Subtle scroll-triggered fade-ins
- Rounded corners (12–24px) on cards and images
- Delicate hover lifts on portfolio and service cards
